HCDE and ACMS

HCDE

Researching, Designing, & Engineering interactions between humans and technology

What they do:

  • User research studies
  • Designing/Implementing information visualizations
  • Web development & applications

HCDE vs INFO:

  • Informatics = information/people/technology
  • HCDE = people/technology
  • Design/Engineering focus vs. Social Application focus

Getting in

Admission Stats: 22-23/75-100
Average GPA: 3.2-3.4
Undergraduate Enrollment 96

ACMS

Modeling using quantitative reasoning, mathematical analysis, and computational methods

What they do:

  • Varies by disicpline, mostly
  • Modeling
  • Econ, Bio, Stats, Engineering, Algorithms, among others

ACMS vs INFO:

  • Informatics = Study of information, and its relationship between people and technology.
  • ACMS = Application of mathematical and computational concepts and tools
  • Key difference: ACMS is more geared towards business applications, whereas Informatics emphasizes the social aspect.
